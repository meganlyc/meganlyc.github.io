My research explores the diverse potential applications of food and organic waste materials across architecture, fashion, and art, while envisioning their innovative uses in future contexts. The research begins by analyzing how these materials are currently utilized in contemporary design, and then expands into speculative research, exploring unconventional design solutions through experimental exploration. The final outcome will be presented as an experimental publication, with each page made from different organic materials, showcasing variations in texture, thickness, and form. This format aims to reveal the potential of organic materials in various creative fields and encourages audiences to rethink the possibilities of these materials through the combination of theoretical analysis and tangible material samples. By integrating practical research and speculative creation, the research invites audiences to push beyond conventional material boundaries and imagine how organic materials could shape sustainable design and artistic expression in the future.
